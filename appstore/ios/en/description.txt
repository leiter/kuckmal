Kuckmal - German Public TV Libraries in One App

Discover the wealth of German public broadcasting media libraries in one place. Kuckmal brings together the best content from ARD, ZDF, Arte, 3sat, Phoenix, BR, HR, MDR, NDR, RBB, SR, SWR, WDR, KiKA, and many more broadcasters in one clear, easy-to-use app.

ALL CHANNELS, ONE APP
Instead of switching between different apps, Kuckmal bundles all content together. Search the entire catalog of German public broadcasters with a single search.

EASY NAVIGATION
• Browse channels: Select your favorite broadcaster and discover their current offerings
• Explore topics: Find shows by categories like documentaries, news, culture, or entertainment
• Search titles: Look for your favorite show directly

POWERFUL SEARCH
Find exactly what you're looking for. Full-text search covers titles, descriptions, and topics across all available content.

DETAILED INFORMATION
Every show is displayed with complete details:
• Full description
• Duration and broadcast date
• Availability and expiration date
• Direct link to watch

MADE FOR ON-THE-GO
Perfect for mobile use - whether on the train, in a waiting room, or relaxing on the couch. The app is optimized for fast loading and simple operation.

PRIVACY FIRST
• No account required
• No registration
• No advertisements
• No tracking services
• Your data stays on your device

SUPPORTED BROADCASTERS
ARD (Das Erste), ZDF, Arte, 3sat, Phoenix, BR (Bavarian Broadcasting), HR (Hessian Broadcasting), MDR (Central German Broadcasting), NDR (North German Broadcasting), RBB (Berlin-Brandenburg Broadcasting), SR (Saarland Broadcasting), SWR (Southwest Broadcasting), WDR (West German Broadcasting), KiKA, funk, and more.

Kuckmal makes the best of German public television accessible - free, ad-free, and privacy-friendly.

Note: Kuckmal is an independent aggregator and is not affiliated with the public broadcasters. All content is provided directly from the official media libraries of the broadcasters.
