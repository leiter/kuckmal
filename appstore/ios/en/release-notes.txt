First release of Kuckmal!

With this app you can:
• Browse all German public broadcasting media libraries
• Explore channels and topics
• Search for shows
• View detailed information for all content

Supported broadcasters: ARD, ZDF, Arte, 3sat, Phoenix, BR, HR, MDR, NDR, RBB, SR, SWR, WDR, KiKA, and more.

Enjoy discovering!
