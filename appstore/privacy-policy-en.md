# Privacy Policy for Kuckmal

*Last updated: February 2026*

## Overview

Kuckmal is an app that aggregates content from German public broadcasting media libraries. Protecting your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Summary

- **No user account required**
- **No personal data collection**
- **No tracking or analytics**
- **All data stays locally on your device**

## Data Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

Kuckmal does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identification data (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Advertising IDs
- Contacts or other personal information

### Local Data Storage

The app stores only the following data locally on your device:

- **App settings**: Your preferred settings within the app
- **Downloaded content**: If you use the offline feature, media files are stored locally

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## Third-Party Content

Kuckmal displays content from the media libraries of German public broadcasters. When you play a video, it is streamed directly from the servers of the respective broadcaster (ARD, ZDF, Arte, etc.). The privacy policies of these broadcasters apply to content playback:

- ARD: [ardmediathek.de/datenschutz](https://www.ardmediathek.de/datenschutz)
- ZDF: [zdf.de/datenschutz](https://www.zdf.de/datenschutz)
- Arte: [arte.tv/de/datenschutzerklaerung](https://www.arte.tv/de/datenschutzerklaerung)

## No Advertising

Kuckmal contains no advertisements and no advertising SDKs. No data is transmitted to advertising networks.

## No Analytics Tools

We do not use analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services. We do not collect usage statistics.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no risks regarding the security of your personal data through our app.

## GDPR Compliance

Kuckmal complies with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Data minimization**: We do not collect personal data
- **Purpose limitation**: No data is used for other purposes
- **Storage limitation**: Local data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app
- **No data transfer**: There is no transfer to third countries

### Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, the typical GDPR rights such as access, rectification, or deletion do not apply. All locally stored data can be completely removed by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this privacy policy. Changes will be published in the app or on our website.
