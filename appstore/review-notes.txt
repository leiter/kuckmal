Notes for Apple Review Team
============================

APP OVERVIEW
Kuckmal is an aggregator app for German public broadcasting media libraries (Mediatheken). It provides a unified interface to browse and search content from ARD, ZDF, Arte, 3sat, Phoenix, and other German public broadcasters.

NO LOGIN REQUIRED
- The app does not require any user account or login
- All features are accessible immediately upon launch
- No demo account is needed for testing

CONTENT SOURCE
- All content comes from publicly available APIs of German public broadcasters
- The broadcasters (ARD, ZDF, etc.) are publicly funded institutions similar to PBS/NPR in the US or BBC in the UK
- Content is freely available to anyone in Germany and many programs worldwide
- The app aggregates publicly accessible metadata and provides direct links to the broadcaster streaming servers

HOW TO TEST
1. Launch the app
2. Browse channels by selecting any broadcaster (e.g., ARD, ZDF)
3. Use the search feature to find specific shows
4. Tap on any item to see detailed information
5. Links open the content in the respective broadcaster's player

DATA & PRIVACY
- No user data is collected
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertisements
- All data stays on device

LANGUAGE
- The app interface supports German and English
- Content is primarily in German as it comes from German broadcasters
